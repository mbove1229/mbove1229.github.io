Welcome to My Van Build!

In 2020 when COVID-19 hit, I moved back home to California with my family. During that time I decided to convert a used cargo van into a fully functioning camper that I could live and travel in. I built everything myself, from the electrical wiring to the plumbing system to the wood interior. This site walks through each part of that process.

Van Build Overview

  • Electrical
    • 360W solar panels on the roof
    • 100Ah lithium battery bank
    • Shore power and DC/DC charger
    • Interior lighting
  • Plumbing
    • 25 gallong fresh water tank
    • Electric water pump
    • Gray water tank under the sink
    • Instant hot water outdoor shower
  • Woodwork and Design
    • Custom cabinets for storage
    • Cedar siding
    • Burlap siding behind bed
    • Elevated bed for more storage
